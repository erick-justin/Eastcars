Our Services

Over the years we have focused on educating people, and by 2021 our Audience demanded more premium services in the name of getting quality education and other services associated with Automobiles.

Online Membership Forum

This is an Online forum with paid membership with the aim to bring car owners and others that are planning to own a car closer to each other so that they can share experties and opinions and also get direct consultation from car experts.

Workshop

Our Members can now bring their cars at our workshop for general maintance, repair and learn few things about their cars.

Mobile App consultation

We have developed a mobile application Called Casandra that recommeds to people list of cars that they can afford based on their lifestyle and incomes. Also they can order spare parts, read articles and watch car reviews from the platform

Vehicle Inspection

We also help people to inspect vehicles before they purchase them to determine the condition of the vehicle.

Vehicle Imports

This service is strictly for our members only, we help them import all kinds of vehicles from different parts of the world like Japan, United kingdom, Austarlia, South Africa, Dubai etc.

Projects
